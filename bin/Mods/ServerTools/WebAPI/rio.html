2 to 4 players
Each player is assigned their own color
The first player to claim four squares in a row horizontally or vertically wins

Instructions:

Players take turns trying to match their dice with the various sequences on the board
When their dice match a sequence, it will highlight grey and can be claimed by clicking on it. The square will change to their colour
Each player gets up to three rolls per turn. Dice can be held between rolls by clicking them
The player can claim a square at any time during their turn, but they must claim a square at the end of three rolls if possible

Sequences:

A straight contains at least one die matching each number from a set. 1 2 3 or 4 5 6
A full house contains three of a kind and two of a kind
Super 7 is the total of all dice equaling 7
Lucky 11 is the total of all dice equaling 11
Five of a kind is special and will let the player take any square including one already claimed by another player
Wild can be used by any player to connect four of their claimed squares in a row
All other sequences are shown on the squares
